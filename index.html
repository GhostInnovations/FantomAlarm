FANTOM — fire detection and tracking

Scroll to reveal

Built for fast response

From detection to actionable fire data in minutes.

FANTOM is designed to detect fires in remote, rural and urban areas using infrared technology. When a fire is detected, the system sends live information including location, temperature, speed and spread to the mobile app.

The app combines sensor data with terrain and wind conditions to visualise the fire area and project likely movement, giving teams a clearer view before they arrive on site.

How it works

A simple field network: one trunk, multiple leaves.

Trunk base node

The trunk contains fold-out solar cells, a lithium battery, infrared sensor, GPS tracker and Bluetooth receptor. It collects data from leaf nodes and relays it to the app.

Leaf nodes

Leaf nodes are smaller solar-powered units that can be placed across a wider area. Each leaf can sync to the trunk or to another nearby leaf.

Mesh coverage

Nodes can sync within roughly 1.5 km, creating a scalable detection net. More leaf nodes increase coverage and improve the quality of fire movement data.

App preview

Clear map outputs for detection points and predicted spread.

The app shows current detection points, stored GPS history, the detected fire area and projected spread based on environmental conditions.
