Progress

FANTOM development timeline

Key milestones from concept research through prototype development, app creation and planned field testing.

December 2018

Initial idea

Brainstorming sessions explored current fire response limitations, available technology and potential approaches for faster detection.

September 2019

Market research

Early research focused on technology fit, market need and the potential scalability of a fire detection network.

November 2020

Research and solutions

Ghost Innovations commissioned reports covering current methods, technical requirements, potential solutions, products and parts.

February 2021

Infrared detection

Ghost Innovations worked with a local prototype company to develop the custom infrared laser required for field detection.

December 2023

Trunk prototype

The first base node prototype was outsourced to a local manufacturing company.

June 2024

FANTOM app

The development team created the FANTOM app for map output, detection points, GPS history and spread visualisation.

Upcoming

Leaf prototype

Ghost Innovations plans to outsource the build of three leaf node prototypes.

Upcoming

System testing

The complete system will be tested across different locations and conditions.

Upcoming

Multiple prototypes

Thirty additional leaf node prototypes are planned to increase testing coverage.

Upcoming

Large-scale testing

Ghost Innovations plans a 12-month woodland test over a 10-acre area.

Upcoming

Manufacture for global implementation

The long-term goal is to manufacture FANTOM alarms for deployment in high-risk areas.
